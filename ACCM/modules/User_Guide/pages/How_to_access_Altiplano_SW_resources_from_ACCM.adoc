= How to access Altiplano SW resources from {app-short-name}

== Introduction

This section provides the steps required to access direct hyperlinks pointing to the resources inside Altiplano from the
{app-short-name} interface.

This allows the operator to seamlessly shift context into the underlying management platform to
troubleshoot localized issues.


[NOTE]
====
Before accessing resources, you must be authenticated to the {app-short-name} as an altiplano user.
Refer to xref:User_Guide:How_to_manage_ACCM_access.adoc[How to Manage {app-short-name} access] section for more details.
====

== Access Altiplano Campaign from {app-short-name} campaign list
To access Altiplano campaign associated to an {app-short-name} campaign using the WebUI,
you must execute the following steps:

. Click the "CAMPAIGNS" option on the menu bar displayed on top of the screen.
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menu.png[align="center"]

. All the campaigns will be listed:
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menuSection.png[align="center"]

. Locate the desired campaign and click the deeplink button on the row:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink.png[align="center"]

. Click the desired cluster in the popup to navigate to Altiplano's campaign:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink_popup_campaign.png[align="center"]

. A new tab with the campaign on Altiplano's site must be shown:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_AltiplanoCampaignDetails.png[align="center"]



== Access Altiplano Campaign from {app-short-name} campaign details
To access Altiplano campaign associated to an {app-short-name} cluster using the WebUI,
you must execute the following steps:

. Click the "CAMPAIGNS" option on the menu bar displayed on top of the screen.
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menu.png[align="center"]

. All the campaigns will be listed:
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menuSection.png[align="center"]

. Locate the campaign that contains the desired cluster, and click the Detail button on the row.
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_detail.png[align="center"]

. All the clusters of the campaign will be listed:
+
.Clusters
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_GeneralProgress.png[align="center"]

. Locate the desired cluster and click the deeplink button on the row.
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink.png[align="center"]

. Click the option in the popup to navigate to Altiplano:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink_popup_cluster.png[align="center"]

. A new tab with the campaign on Altiplano's site must be shown:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_AltiplanoCampaignDetails.png[align="center"]


== Access Altiplano device activity from {app-short-name} campaign cluster details

. Click the "CAMPAIGNS" option on the menu bar displayed on top of the screen.
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menu.png[align="center"]

. All the campaigns will be listed:
+
image::CAMPAIGNS_menuSection.png[align="center"]

. Locate the campaign that contains the desired cluster, and click the Detail button on the row.
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_detail.png[align="center"]


. All the clusters of the campaign will be listed:
+
.Clusters
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_GeneralProgress.png[align="center"]

. Locate the desired cluster and click the Detail button on the row.
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_detail.png[align="center"]

. All the cluster elements will be listed:
+
.Cluster elements
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_clusterDetails2.png[align="center"]

. Locate the desired element of the cluster and click the deeplink button on the row.
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink.png[align="center"]

. Locate the desired activity of cluster element in the popup and click it:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_deeplink_popup.png[align="center"]

. A new tab with the element on Altiplano's site must be shown:
+
image::CAMPAIGNSSection_AltiplanoDeviceDetails.png[align="center"]
